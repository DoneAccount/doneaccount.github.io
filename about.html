Our Story

On May 6, 2023, a quiet corner along B. Mendoza Street, Barangay Sto. Rosario, City of San Fernando, Pampanga came to life. Right in front of Prime Water, a new coffee shop opened its doors. Not with a grand, flashy launch, but with a soft opening that felt more like a warm welcome than a business debut.
The shop was called doze. coffee, a name that hinted at its purpose: a place where you could slow down, take a breather, and maybe even “doze” off into your own thoughts while sipping something warm or iced.
